EASEE Uses an ACTION Approach

Actions speak louder than words

We are serious about having a positive impact on climate change. That is why our efforts focus on changing individual behavior. The educational approach alone doesn't guarantee change, but education coupled with empowerment does make a difference. It's the resulting actions that we foster early in children's lives that lead them to behave responsibly throughout their lifetime. 

Our first project provides two CFLs to each grammar school student to be used in their homes. This requires them to teach their family members what they have learned from older students and, more importantly, to act upon what they've learned. Students will return to school with a form signed by an adult to validate the installation of each bulb. It is the recording and authentication process that further differentiates EASEE from other programs, and reflects the lessons learned over a period of time working informally in the schools to promote energy-saving awareness.
