EASEE Focuses on Sustainability

__________________________________________________________________________________________

Sustainability asks that we take the long view of our actions. 


What we do, how we do it, and the calculations we make do matter, especially when it comes to climate change. For that reason we've chosen a holistic approach – one that connects people, policies, and practices. Our focus and actions are concentrated locally on the things we can do in our own homes and community. 

 

Our approach helps people understand that some of their current behavior is akin to taking a short cut, and we want to help them understand the consequences down the road. The best way to effect change is to help children and young adults take control of the future by giving them the knowledge and the means to make a difference. The key to becoming more sustainable is education. The more we know, the more we can do to make a difference. 

 

Our approach is geared to help children and young adults realize the impact they have on the world around them, beginning with their home environment. We begin by breaking down our daily routine into a series of choices and actions and demonstrate how we affect the world around us on a much larger scale than we’d like to think. Learning to turn off a light when we leave the room, unplug rarely-used appliances when not in use, and use CFLs, which use only a fraction of the electricity that their incandescent equivalents do, are important first steps to take to improve our environment. Reducing waste not only saves limited natural resources but also saves our families money.
