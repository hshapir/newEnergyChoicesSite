EASEE and the Southside Energy Efficiency District (SEED) 

____________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________
Southside Chicago communities working together to save money, protect the environment and educate our kids 


(Click here to go immediately to instructions for joining SEED)


In seeking to expand the reach of our educational programs, EASEE has teamed up with one of our sponsors, the energy consulting firm Energy Choices, P.C., to implement an exciting endeavor---the Southside Energy Efficiency District (SEED). The mission of SEED is to develop and operate an economically sustainable community-wide enterprise that helps residents and businesses in Chicago’s Southside communities take proactive measures to improve the environment and save money on utilities bills. Funding for SEED results from the direct access its members receive to electricity suppliers outside of ComEd (Illinois’ default supplier), which allows these members, both residents and small businesses, to purchase their electricity at reduced rates. Energy Choices, acting as Integrys’ sole agent in the City of Chicago for residential and small business customers, will donate 25% of the commission revenue from this agreement to EASEE to enable us to run the SEED program.

The heart of SEED is its community focus. Every member of the program will benefit economically from reduced energy costs and will be encouraged to take part in projects that raise environmental awareness in Southside communities. Together, families will learn that becoming energy efficient is straightforward and results in economic savings as well as an improved climate. To begin building this environmentally-aware district, EASEE will implement our educational and CFL-Distribution programs in CPS elementary schools and food pantries located in participating Southside communities. Future projects include partnering with other community organizations to coordinate home audits for SEED members, which will help families pinpoint ways they can save energy in their households. For instance, many families can reduce energy usage by weather stripping (to prevent air leaks), using their existing appliances more efficiently, and taking advantage of deals and promotions making energy efficient appliances more affordable. 

As is true with our other programs, one of EASEE’s goals in this endeavor is to empower young people by involving them in our environmental education efforts. In addition to bringing our curriculum to elementary, middle, and high school students in SEED’s communities, we will also include many of these students in our food pantry visits and plan to teach them to help in undertaking home audits in their neighborhoods. 

 Strengthen Your Community By Joining SEED

  • Help to create a healthier earth by using less energy and reducing greenhouse gas emissions, which will slow the effects of climate change;

  • Contribute to environmental education programs that benefit everyone in your community.
